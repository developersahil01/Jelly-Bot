What We Do & Why We Do

Our general goal is to address certain social or environmental challenges that the government or the corporate sector are not sufficiently addressing. We assist and support communities who are in need. We frequently concentrate on assisting individuals who are underprivileged or disenfranchised, such as those who are homeless, refugees, or those who have been impacted by natural disasters or war.

We also work to fill service gaps left by the government we are frequently more adaptable and sensitive to community demands than government institutions and we can offer essential services in places where the government does not have the means or capability to do so.
